Webring is basically site that linked to each other

[work in progress]

A webring (or web ring) is a collection of websites that are all linked together in a circle. They were popular in the 1990s and early 2000s, before search engines were as good as they are today.

Webrings are a great way to find websites that are related to your interests. They are also a good way to get traffic to your own website. When you join a webring, your website will be linked to all of the other websites in the ring. This means that people who are visiting other websites in the ring may also visit your website.
